Widely regarded as one of the greatest artists of all time, Kanye West has had a lucrative musical career full of successful hits.

Let's take a look at his top 5 songs:

  1. Runaway

  2. Heartless

  3. Father Stretch My Hands Pt. 1

  4. Flashing Lights

  5. Gold Digger

In first place, I have Runaway from My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy. In this track, Kanye talks about his high opinion of himself and how he will likely never change, and that those close to him should "run away" or else risk being hurt by him.


In second place, I have Heartless from 808s & Heartbreak. In this track, Kanye reflects upon a toxic relationship he was once trapped in as well as how we deal with loss.


In third place, I have Father Stretch My Hands Pt. 1 from Life of Pablo. This track was an ode to his father, Ray West, who divorced Kanye's mother Donda over financial issues, as the song suggests.


In fourth place, I have Flashing Lights from Graduation. In this track, Kanye speaks about the struggles of being famous and the fast-paced lifestyle that accompanies fame. He also speaks about how his relationships have been strained by his glamorous lifestyle.


Last but certainly not least, I have Gold Digger from Late Registration. In this track, Kanye talks about "gold diggers," which are women who only want men for their money and will often use deception to accomplish her goals. Kanye warns against gold diggers and preaches practicing caution when it comes to mixing women with money.
